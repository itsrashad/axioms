WHATâ€™S OUR PLAN?

Axiom is a unique decentralized crypto project that is aiming to automate airdrops, a key element in scaling blockchain projects. We will deploy a platform powered by our own cryptocurrency. This platform will enable partner projects to grow their social media communities while automating reward mechanisms. Airdrops will be done by our unique X-Swaps system, a system for cross-chain atomic swaps that leverages masternodes for transaction verification.

Axiom cryptocurrency holders will be eligible to participate in airdrop campaigns from partner projects. The value of the project, therefore, relies on its strong network effects: on one side, establishing partnerships with high-potential projects; on the other, creating a valuable and active community of Axiom holders that projects perceive as highly-valuable to scale their projects.
